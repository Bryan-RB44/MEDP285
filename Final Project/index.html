About us

Our site The Old Save File is the place to be at for anything related to the history of major video game companies over the past decade. We have information on Nintendo, Sega, Playstation, and Microsoft alongside links to further information such as consoles and games via Wikipedia. This site intends to store information due to crackdowns on video game history via emulator and rom site take downs, so as a loophole we only provide information on these console makers and music samples from some of their games visualized via the p5.js library. Enjoy your stay and hope you learn something new about this small portion of video game history!
