Our Mission:

Are you tired of dealing with classes that you need being automatically filled up as soon as your enrollment date comes? Are you also sick of having to bear with courses you'd never otherwise take? Have you also needed to change a class after that "Staff" turns into an infamous professor's name in the course description?

Worry no more! This site is dedicated to the students, by the students! Find listings from people willing to sell their positions in courses you're in desperate need for at the non-official Hunter College Course Marketplace!

Here at The Hunter College Course Marketplace, we host listings for anyone's needs, ranging from major-related classes to even listings which are from other CUNY schools so everyone across CUNY can become involved! We also offer flat-fee seat services to find courses for you if no listing matches what you're looking for!

About

Satire-aside, this website serves as commentary to the struggle that students at Hunter College face when it comes to obtaining classes that they need in their academic careers.

Whether it's being beat by honor students , landing a bad enrollment time on enrollment day, and/or there simply being too many students in the semester, this site is meant to represent that sense of frustration and desperation in students. While it's intentionally written less formally, it is meant to show that it's still very well likely that some might turn to alternative means for money and others for courses if need be.
