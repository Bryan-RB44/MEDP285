Brief History of the Color Lavender

Lavender is a lighter shade of violet/purple, but that doesn't take away from how unique this color is. This shade of purple can range from leaning towards being blue-ish, gray-ish, pink-ish, and even different tones which range in how dark and light it can get. Most of the time though, a color is generally considered lavender as long as its a pale looking version of purple.
